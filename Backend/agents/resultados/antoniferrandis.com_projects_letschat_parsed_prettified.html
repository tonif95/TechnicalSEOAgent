Meet My Personal AI Assistant

Visit Website tszhong0411 / friend-quiz

Want to interact directly with me and learn more about my background, skills, and experience? I've developed a conversational AI assistant powered by GPT-4o-mini and Gradio that acts as my digital double right here on this website.

This innovative system goes beyond a traditional chatbot:

Intelligent Responses: Access detailed information about my professional summary and LinkedIn profile, all processed by the language model to provide you with precise and contextualized answers. Interaction Management: If a question goes unanswered, the system automatically logs it for my review. Plus, it can request and save your contact details (name and email) if you'd like me to get in touch with you, all through secure and efficient tool usage (record_user_details and record_unknown_question). Real-time Notifications: Receive instant notifications via Pushover every time a user detail or unanswered question is logged, allowing me to follow up proactively. This project demonstrates my ability to integrate advanced language models, develop interactive interfaces (Gradio), and create automated solutions (Python, APIs) to enhance communication and information management. I invite you to try it out and discover how my expertise can benefit your team!
